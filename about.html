About ToyoToys

Creating magical moments and happy memories through the power of play

Our Story

How It All Began

ToyoToys was founded in 2010 by parents Sarah and Michael Johnson, who were frustrated with the lack of high-quality, safe, and educational toys on the market. As parents of three young children, they understood firsthand the importance of play in a child's development.

What started as a small online store operating from their garage has now grown into a beloved brand with multiple retail locations across the country and a thriving e-commerce platform serving families worldwide.

Our Growth Journey

Over the years, ToyoToys has expanded its product range from just 50 items to over 2,000 carefully curated toys, games, and educational products. We've built partnerships with over 200 toy manufacturers who share our commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability.

In 2018, we launched our innovative "Toy Box Subscription" service, which delivers age-appropriate toys to families every month, helping children discover new interests and skills through play.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide children with high-quality, safe, and educational toys that inspire creativity, foster development, and create joyful memories. We believe in the power of play to shape young minds and build a foundation for lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted and beloved toy brand, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and child development. We envision a future where every child has access to toys that nurture their unique talents and interests.

Safety First

All our products meet or exceed the highest safety standards, ensuring peace of mind for parents.

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices and sustainable materials in our products and packaging.

Educational Value

We carefully select toys that promote learning, creativity, and developmental skills.

Passion for Play

We believe in the transformative power of play and its essential role in childhood.

Our Journey

2010

Company Founded

Eeshan,Talha,Hamza Mahad launch ToyoToys from the road with just 50 carefully selected products.

2012

First Retail Store

We open our first brick-and-mortar store in San Francisco, bringing our curated toy collection to local families.

2015

National Expansion

ToyoToys expands to 10 retail locations across the United States, with our e-commerce platform reaching all 50 states.

2018

Toy Box Subscription Launch

We introduce our innovative subscription service, delivering age-appropriate toys to thousands of families monthly.

2020

International Growth

Despite global challenges, we expand to Canada, the UK, and Australia, bringing joy to children worldwide.

2023

Sustainability Initiative

We launch our comprehensive eco-friendly program, committing to 100% sustainable packaging by 2025.
